Hey there!

I’m Analysa Frazier, and my mission is to help women achieve optimal fertility.

Fertility is a sign of health for women. It’s not just about having babies. It's about allowing our bodies to do what they were meant to do, which is conceive. I teach women how to do this through my services and programs by showing them that you don't have to “diet” to reach your goals. You also don't have to do vigorous workouts 5 days a week. Living a healthy and balanced lifestyle is what I believe in. You can still eat chocolate and drink wine. Girl, I LOVE me some good wine and chocolate and I would NEVER take that away from you! Instead, let me show you how you can still travel, go out and live your life while still reaching your goals.
